Abhaneri

Samrat Mihir Bhoj, who was the Gurjar Pratihar King, is believed to have founded the village of Abhaneri. It was originally named Abha Nagri, meaning the “city of brilliance,” but with time it was changed to Abhaneri because of the mispronunciation of the name.

Address: Abhaneri is a village in the Rajasthan district of Dausa, located in the distance 95 km from Jaipur on Jaipur-Agra Highway
